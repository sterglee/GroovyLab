VD =  eig(A)

Computes the eigenvalues ëj and eigenvectors u(j) (j=1,...,n) of an nXn real matrix A. 

For an NXN matrix A the returned matrix VD is of dimension NX(N+2).


VD(1:N, N+1:N+2)
  the real part and the imaginary part of the calculated eigenvalues are delivered in the N+1 and N+2 columns respctively, i.e. VD(1:N, N+1) has the real parts and VD(1:N, N+2) the imaginary

VD(1:N, 1:N)
  the eigenvectors of the matrix; 
an eigenvector corresponding to a real eigenvalue given in array VD(1:N, N+1)  is de livered in the corresponding column of array VD(1:N, 1:N); the real and imaginary part of an eigenvector corresponding to the first member of a nonreal conjugate pair of eigenvalues given in arrays re, and im are delivered in the two consecutive columns of array VD(1:N, 1:N) corresponding to this pair
( the eigenvectors corresponding to the second members of nonreal complex conjugate pairs are not delivered, since they are simply the complex conjugate of those corresponding to the first member of such pairs ).
